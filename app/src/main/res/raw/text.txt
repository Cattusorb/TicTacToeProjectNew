Tic Tac Go is a android variation on the game Tic Tac Toe or more generally known Xs and Os.
The way to win in Tic Tac Go is simple, get three Xs or Os in a row depending on what side you are playing.
The goal of the game is also to prevent the opponent from getting three in a row as well.
Each player will take turns placing their tile until either player X or player O gets three in a row, or
all the spaces in the 3x3 grid are filled, this will result in a tie.
Upon completion of a game of TicTacGo! you will see displayed either "X wins!", "O wins!", or "Tie game!". After this happens
you will have the ability to click "back to home", or "rematch".
